Follow the following steps to download geogebra applets:

Step 1: Click on the download button.

Step 2: Click on the chapter name button.In following photo,Statistics is being clicked.

Step 3: Click on the topic name button.After clicking the topick name, you have to wait few seconds for the file to be opened.In following photo, find missing frequency when the mean is given is being clicked.

Step 4: Click on the black circled three horizontal line button.

Step 5: Click on the black circled download as button.

Step 6: Click on the black circled GeoGebra file(.ggb) button.

Step 7: Click on the save button.

After following the above steps, the geogebra file will be downloaded in your device(computer, mobile).Then you can use this file to teach your students.
