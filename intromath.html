What is Mathematics?

  • The word Mathematics is derived from the Greek word máthēma which means “knowledge” or “that which is learnt”.

  • Mathematics is a universal language and science concerning with numbers, logic, shapes, measuring, computing/calculating, analyzing and interpreting.It includes the study of quantity, structure, space and change.

  • Mathematics is a language which use symbols notations for describing numerical, geometrical and graphical relationships.Because of this the lengthy statement can be expressed in brief/short form.Thus, Mathematics is the process of abstraction followed by symbolization and learning the rule of using symbols.

  • Mathematics is the study of patterns: notice patterns, describe patterns, generalize patterns.

  • Mathematics is everywhere in nature and in everything we do. It is the building block for everything in our daily lives including mobile devices, architecture, sports etc.
