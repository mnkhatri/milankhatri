What is the importance of Mathematics in our life?

  • We are using Mathematics daily without even realizing that it is everywhere in nature and in everything that we do. It is the building block for everything in our daily lives, including mobile devices, architecture,sports etc. It is used in cooking, making budget, shoping, maintaining healthy life style, money exchange, time management, tendering process,calculating rates, forcasting weather, measuring the distance between planets, research etc.Moreover, the engineers, computer programmer, accountants, economist, banker, scientists etc. use mathematics.

  • Mathematics is the foundation of every science: physics, chemistry, biology etc. Moreover, It helps to understand the other subjects such as statistics, social, music, art, economics, account etc.

  • Mathematics develops the ability of logical reasoning, critical and analytical thinking, remembering, imagining, understanding, analizing, generating ideas, effective communication, problem solving etc. Thus, it helps us better understand and describe the world around us and efficiently deal with the life challenges. It quickens our mind and increases the speed of intuition. So that, we become smart and get smart job,which makes our life easier and beautiful.

  • Mathematics is necesssary in doing all kind of new things, innovations, inventions and discoveries,which helps in the evolution of the world.
