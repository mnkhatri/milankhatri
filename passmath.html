How to be good at Mathematics and pass Mathematics?

    Follow the following 8 steps to be good at math and pass math:
  1. Be motivated:

    Think that “I need to pass the exam to move forward in life”.

  2. Develop positive attitudes:

    Think that “I can definitely pass the exam if I continuously practice math from today to the day of exam. Practicing math not only makes me able to pass the exam but also gives me enjoyment like playing a game”.

  3. Make a determination:

    Think that “I will try my best to pass the exam in first attempt. I will practice math daily. I will not leave practicing math by making any excuse.”

  4. Focus on your classroom study:

    Be silent and focus on your classroom study. If you have any doubt during the teaching and learning process in classroom, then ask question to the teacher or friends and be clear on the spot.

  5. Practice solving the problems:

    Do complete your homework. Try to solve new problems from reference books using different ideas to increase your knowledge.

  6. Clear your doubts that you have while practicing math:

    If you have any problem while doing homework or practicing new problems, then choose the best option {books, teachers, friends, seniors, Internet (youtube, google, facebook etc.)} to solve the problem.

  7. Be dedicated:

    Practice math daily as often as possible for being good at math. More practice makes you more good at math.

  8. Be patient:

    Even if you are not feeling that you are being good at math after continuous practice of math, don’t give up practicing math. Being good at math takes longer time for some people. Be patient and continue practicing math. One day, you will definitely be good at math.
