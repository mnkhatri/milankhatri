How to download geogebra software for computer?

Follow the following steps to download geogebra software for computer:

Step 1: Open the browser (Google Chrome,Opera Mini,etc.) that you have installed in your computer.

Step 2: Type "download geogebra" on the search box,then click on the "search" option.

Step 3: Click on the link "Download GeoGebra Apps".

Step 4: Click on the "download" option situated below the box "GeoGebra Classic 5".

Step 5: Click on the "save" option.

After installing the geogebra software,you can run that to make and open the geogebra applets.
